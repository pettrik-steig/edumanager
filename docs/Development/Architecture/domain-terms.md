# Domain Terms

This file collects central German domain terms from the old Sitzplan project
and early EduManager analysis.

The descriptions are intentionally non-technical. They describe the school
domain meaning, not implementation details.

## Initial Term List

- **Belegung**: the fact that a seat is occupied by or assigned to a student.
- **Halbjahr**: one of the two major subdivisions of a school year.
- **Jahrgang**: a school year level grouping students by grade level.
- **Klasse**: an organizational school class, usually more administrative than
  a concrete teaching group.
- **Kollege**: another teacher or school staff member involved in information
  exchange.
- **Kurs**: an organizational teaching unit, for example in a timetable,
  described by information such as teacher, subject, year level, and context.
- **Kurszuteilung**: the assignment of students to a course or teaching group.
- **Lehrer**: the teacher using or maintaining information for teaching work.
- **Lerngruppe**: the group of students assigned to a course or teaching
  context.
- **Oberstufenkurs**: an upper-secondary course that organizes teaching for
  students who may not share one fixed class group.
- **Raum**: a physical room used for teaching or school work.
- **Raumsituation**: the concrete usable setup of a room in a teaching
  situation.
- **Raumwechsel**: a change from one room to another for a teaching situation.
- **Schueler**: a student as an individual person relevant to teaching,
  learning groups, seating, and classroom work.
- **Schuelerbestand**: the known set of students available for the current work
  context.
- **Schuelerdaten**: information describing a student for school and teaching
  work.
- **Schuelerliste**: a named list of students with a specific practical
  purpose.
- **Schule**: the school organization as a source, owner, or context of
  official information.
- **Schuljahr**: the yearly school period that structures recurring preparation
  and maintenance work.
- **Sitzordnung**: an assignment of students to seats for a learning group and
  room situation.
- **Sitzplan**: a visual or practical representation of a seating arrangement
  for classroom use.
- **Sitzplatz**: a place where one student can sit in a room situation.
- **Sitzplatzqualitaet**: a qualitative property of a seat that can matter for
  classroom decisions.
- **Stammdaten**: relatively stable basic information about a person or school
  object.
- **Stundenplan**: the timetable context describing when teaching situations
  take place.
- **Unterricht**: the concrete teaching activity or lesson context.
- **Vertretung**: a temporary teaching situation handled differently from the
  usual plan.
- **Zuweisung**: the assignment of a person or object to a group, context, or
  role.
